About Doaba Group of Colleges

“Where enlightenment leads to empowerment”

Ever since its inception in 1998, the Doaba Group of Colleges (DGC) has evolved into a center of academic excellence in North India. The institution focuses on delivering not just knowledge but also values, leadership, and real-world skills.

DGC offers diverse programs including engineering, management, computer applications, pharmacy, agriculture, and humanities. With strong industry connections and modern infrastructure, students receive both academic and practical exposure.

Guided by the Doaba Khalsa Trust, the college promotes innovation, creativity, and holistic development. The goal is to empower students with the skills and confidence to succeed in their careers.

We aim to develop skilled professionals with strong academic knowledge and practical experience.

Mohali Campus

Just 13 kms from Chandigarh (on Chandigarh – Ropar Highway, Midway Kharar – Kurali), spread over 30 acres. Easily accessible from Himachal Pradesh, Haryana, Jammu, and Delhi.

Nawanshahar Campus

Just 7 kms from Nawanshahar on Rahon – Chandigarh Road, Distt- Nawanshahar.

Ropar Campus

Located at Raipur, 13 kms from Ropar on Nawanshahr–Ropar Highway.

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a transparent, efficient, and fully digital admission process that simplifies the experience for students and institutions. We aim to eliminate paperwork, reduce delays, and ensure secure handling of student data. By leveraging modern technology, we strive to create a seamless and user-friendly platform that supports students at every step of their academic journey.

🚀 Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading digital education management system that transforms traditional processes into smart, automated solutions. We aspire to build a platform that connects students, colleges, and administrators in real-time, ensuring better communication, faster decision-making, and improved accessibility. In the future, we aim to integrate advanced technologies to create a fully smart and connected educational ecosystem.
